Biography / 自傳

Hi, my name is Herbert. I am very passionate about the latest technologies and everything related to the computer and the Internet. Constantly learning and overcoming challenges are exciting to me, so I always strive to learn different skills and technologies. I see myself as a flexible team player with humor, but when necessary I will firmly stick to my principle if I believe that is for the best.

I studied in Hong Kong University of Science and Technology(HKUST) for my bachelor degree. Although the academic resources there were quite helpful, I feel that my biggest takeaway from my 4 years there is to work on what I am passionate about and pursue excellence.

I am actually a man of multiple interests. Be it work or hobby, I tend to give it my all. When working as a web developer, I often come across new frameworks that I need to use but I am unfamiliar with. I will try to piece together different components and read its documentation intensive until I build something. When it comes to my hobby I like to do as much related reading as possible to hone my craft.

In my previous experiences, while I prefer to do my own thinking when solving a problem, I also fit well in a team setting. I can engage in discussions and bring out my own perspective on the matter as a solution to problems. If there isn’t a leader present I can lead but otherwise I am happy to provide support for the sake of the team.

I’m always happy to have a conversation, no matter whether it’s work-related or non-work-related. Feel free to send me a message. Sharing experiences is exciting!
